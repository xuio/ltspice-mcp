# Code of Conduct

This project aims to be a friendly, professional place for collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on code, behavior, and technical tradeoffs.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating.
- Share reproducible details when reporting bugs.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Doxxing, threats, or intimidation.
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or review processes.
- Bad-faith bug reports or intentionally misleading technical claims.

## Scope

This applies to project spaces, including issues, pull requests, and discussions related to this repository.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content that violates this policy and may restrict participation for repeated or severe violations.

## Reporting

If you encounter a conduct issue, open a private issue or contact maintainers through a direct channel you already have available.
